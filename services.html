Check out our Services

Our team is with you from start to finish, whether it be behind the scenes, making sure your adventure is the best it can possibly be, or physically there with you, being your travel companion and assistant as you forge your own adventure!

One of the services we plan to offer is sending one of our professionally trained photographers to come along with you on your journey and make sure you can keep your memorable experiences with you forever.

One of the services we plan to introduce is making sure the transport to your destination is made as easy as possible. With our accessibility friendly vehicles on hand, you'll be up and at 'em in no time!

Buddy System

Do you want to go on a tour but feel you may need extra support? No problem! Sign up to our buddy system here and link up with an able-bodied traveller on your trip. These likeminded travellers are keen to assist you with making sure your daily needs are met and your trip is as smooth as it can be. Who knows, with any luck, you may even meet your future bestie! Alternatively if you are an able-bodied traveller and would love a new pal for your trip please click here.

Why it's awesome

—We make sure that if you're nervous going on your own to a new place on a new adventure, that you always have someone with you

—You very well may find a new best friend in your new travel partner!

—Double the people, double the photos!

Workshop

Are you super keen to go on an adventure but you’re unsure if you are ready to take the leap? We totally understand how daunting it is to do anything for the first time, especially with a group of people you are yet to meet. But don’t worry,we are here to help you keep your focus on what’s important about any adventure - having fun! We hold regular confidence workshops at our Melbourne HQ planning a bunch of easy and fun activities that allow you to engage with other disabled travellers who have taken the leap and never looked back! If you’re not Oz based, no worries, the talks will be posted on our YouTube channel!

Why it's awesome

—We do the research for you, so all you have to do is come along and hang out with us to learn what you need to know!

—Our workshops are held and presented by people who have been through exactly what you have and will go through, so rest assured!

—You can have the opportunity to ask questions to us and learn more about what we do in our offices!

Photography

One of the services we're excited to add to our arsenal is being able to send a professional photographer along with you and your adventure group to make sure your adventure is well documented and something you can keep with you for a long time to come! With one of our founders being a photographer and videographer by trade, you can rest assured that you'll be in good hands!

Why it's awesome

— Keep your adventuring memories with you wherever you go!

— We'll send you a folder either over the cloud or on a USB drive so you can do with your pictures what you want!

— Perfect photos to put on your Instagram story or Facebook wall!
