F.A.Q

If I have a temporary disability that I suddenly need requirements for, can you help me?

Yes we can! Just send us an email with your injury and the amendments you'll need and we'll take it from there!

If I have a carer/ friend accompanying me on my trip, can they book through you aswell?

Yes! We are here to cater for you and your party so you can all have the best experience possible!

Do you cater for intellectual disabilities aswell?

No, unfortunately we are only able to provide assistance for physical requirements but if you have enquiries we can point you towards someone who can help!

What are the benefits of booking through you versus doing it independently?

Stress-free booking is what we're about! We don't want you to have any negative experience in your trip so we are here to make sure everything gets booked without hassle!

If I have an emergency on a trip, can I contact you?

You can contact us during work hours (9am-5pm Melbourne time)

Is there any information package I can download?

We currently have our best people compiling a pamphlet outlining all the services we offer and a little bit about us as a company, so look forward to that!
