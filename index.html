Welcome to Accessible Adventures

Buddy System

Here at Accessible Adventures, one of our main selling points is unparallelled Buddy System service. This service allows people who are nervous about going on an adventure to meet someone else doing the same thing! Just opt in at Sign Up and we'll get you sorted!

Workshops

As we're building up our experience, we're giving you the opportunity to build up yours! In the coming months we plan to hold a variety of workshops in our Melbourne-based office space. If you're not in Melbourne, no worries! Every workshop will be livestreamed on Facebook or Instagram.

Photography

One future service we plan to offer is to bring a photographer with you on your adventure to capture every perfect moment as it happens and let you pick and choose what bits of your adventure you want to bring home and add to your very own Wall of Glory!

Adventure Adaptation

Our core service, of course, is to help you get the most out of your adventure holiday by acting as the go-between with you and the travel agent, so we can make sure you have the best adventure of your life!

About the Company

Accessible Adventures is your new portkey to adventure! We adapt already existing adventures to suit your needs, whether you have mobility issues, are hearing or visually impaired or have an invisible illness, we are here for you. Always wanted to go on that Vietnamese excursion but just couldn't find a way to do it? Well we're here to change that!

Where we started

Accessible Adventures was born out of a passion project created by Jenny Curran and Charlie Parish. After both of them had their fair share of misfortune at the hands of their medical issues when they went on adventure holidays, they decided to team up so that others in their position wouldn't have to experience anything like it again.

Who we are

Accessible Adventures is the brain-child of keen adventurers, Jenny Curran and Charlie Parish. Having travelled to over 70 countries between them, they are excited to change the travel world for the better, one adventure at a time.

Accessible Adventures is run by a passionate team of individuals each with their own experiences of adventuring to help you get the most out of your once-in-a-lifetime experience.
